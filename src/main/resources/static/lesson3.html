Lesson 3: Encapsulation

Concept: OOP - Encapsulation

Encapsulation means bundling data and behavior together while protecting internal state from direct outside access.

What to remember

  • Use private fields to hide internal state.
  • Expose controlled access through getters and setters.
  • Use final to prevent method overriding when needed.
  • Encapsulation improves maintainability and reduces accidental misuse.
